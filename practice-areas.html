Practice Areas

Fifteen disciplines, one point of accountability.

Our practice groups are organised into three clusters — corporate & commercial, disputes & regulatory, and sector focus — each led directly by partners with deep, matter-tested expertise.

Group 01

Corporate & Commercial

No. 01

Mergers & Acquisitions

End-to-end counsel on domestic and cross-border M&A, from structuring through signing and closing.

No. 02

Private Equity & Venture Capital

Advising funds and founders on investment rounds, shareholder arrangements and exits.

No. 03

Capital Markets

IPOs, QIPs, rights issues and debt listings, coordinated closely with regulators and merchant bankers.

No. 04

Banking & Finance

Structuring and documenting syndicated loans, project finance and structured credit facilities.

No. 05

Tax

Transaction tax structuring, transfer pricing strategy and representation before tax authorities.

Group 02

Disputes & Regulatory

No. 06

Competition Law

Merger notifications, cartel defence and abuse-of-dominance proceedings before the CCI.

No. 07

Commercial Litigation

High-stakes contract, shareholder and commercial disputes before Indian courts and tribunals.

No. 08

International Arbitration

Representing parties in institutional and ad hoc arbitrations under Indian and international rules.

No. 09

Insolvency & Restructuring

Corporate insolvency resolution, restructuring and creditor representation under the IBC.

No. 10

White Collar & Investigations

Internal investigations, enforcement defence and advisory on anti-bribery compliance.

No. 11

Employment Law

Advising employers on the labour codes, workforce restructuring and senior-level exits.

Group 03

Sector Focus

No. 12

Real Estate

Land acquisition, RERA compliance and structuring for developers and institutional investors.

No. 13

Technology & Data Privacy

DPDP Act compliance, data localisation strategy and technology transaction advisory.

No. 14

Healthcare & Life Sciences

Regulatory approvals, licensing and transactional support for pharma and healthcare clients.

No. 15

Energy & Infrastructure

Project development, PPAs and regulatory approvals across renewable and conventional energy.
