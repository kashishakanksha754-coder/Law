Our Team

Meet Our Team Members

Our success is driven by a team of dedicated, knowledgeable, and results-oriented legal professionals. Each member of our firm brings a wealth of experience, strategic insight, and a client-focused approach to every case.

Founding Partner & Head, Litigation Practice

Mr. Aditya Saurabh

Mr Aditya Saurabh possesses extensive and comprehensive expertise in the realms of Litigation, Criminal Law, Civil Law, Corporate and Commercial Law, Insolvency and Bankruptcy Laws, Banking and Finance, as well as Real Estate and Municipal Laws. Having earned a degree in International Commercial Law from the University of Nottingham, United Kingdom—a globally esteemed institution—he has applied his legal acumen with increasing sophistication. He has served as a legal advisor to a multitude of corporate entities, including prominent manufacturers and industrial leaders in the Real Estate, Sugar, and Mining sectors. His profound understanding and specialized knowledge of Land Laws, Textiles and Apparel, Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) Laws, and Property Laws, particularly in relation to contractual disputes, have earned him numerous accolades.

Moreover, Mr. Saurabh has a distinguished record of successfully representing esteemed Private Companies and Statutory Corporations in various legal matters before diverse judicial forums. His expertise extends to all facets of Matrimonial and Family Law matters. Additionally, he possesses significant experience in conducting Criminal Trials and resolving Civil Disputes.

Senior Partner · Senior Panel Counsel – UoI – Supreme Court of India

Mr. Animesh Rastogi

Mr. Rastogi is a driving force of MOTANI ASSOCIATES and has been instrumental in developing and nurturing the team. As a team leader Mr. Rastogi has always played a role of a mentor to his team. Mr. Rastogi is enrolled as an Advocate with Delhi Bar Council and has been practicing in the Supreme Court, Delhi High Court, District Courts, Consumer Courts and Central Administrative Tribunal for past 13 years. His areas of specialization are matters related to IPR, civil laws, dispute resolution, and arbitration. He has been actively representing various clients and private organizations before various forums in Delhi and other states. He has a vide exposure and experience of arguing and conducting trials before the Courts and Tribunals. He has an expertise in drafting Civil Suits, Criminal Complaints, Writ Petitions, Special Leave Petitions, and Appeals.

Senior Partner · Senior Panel Counsel – UoI – Supreme Court of India

Mr. Yogendra Verma

Yogendra Kumar Verma is a seasoned legal professional with extensive expertise in Corporate Law, Employment & Labour Law, and Commercial Disputes. He has a proven track record of advising corporations, senior management, and individual clients on complex legal, regulatory, and compliance matters.

With a well-balanced practice spanning both advisory and litigation, he offers practical insights in drafting, regulatory compliance, dispute resolution, and effective courtroom advocacy. His approach combines legal precision with strategic foresight, enabling clients to navigate legal challenges with clarity and confidence.

Mr. Verma has extensive experience in Corporate and Labour Law advisory, including corporate structuring, governance, contract drafting, and regulatory compliance. He also possesses strong expertise in employment matters such as HR policies, service conditions, and workplace disputes. He regularly represents clients in commercial and labour disputes and is adept at drafting legal notices, agreements, and compliance documentation.
