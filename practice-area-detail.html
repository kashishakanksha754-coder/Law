A closer look at each of our ten core practice areas.

Practice Area

Civil & Criminal Litigation

We provide comprehensive legal support to clients involved in civil litigation, covering diverse matters such as recovery disputes, estate and property issues, execution, claims, partitions, and various petitions concerning property laws and other areas. Our team of lawyers approaches disputes with a thorough understanding of the Civil Procedure Code (CPC), which governs civil litigation. We assist with drafting, filing, and representing clients in court, including filing injunctions, seeking reliefs, filing appeals, and representing clients at arraignments and hearings. With our team’s expertise, proficiency, and mastery of the subject matter, we aim to deliver exceptional services to our clients.

Our dispute resolution team has extensive experience in civil and commercial litigation, representing clients before all Judicial and Quasi-Judicial Bodies. Additionally, our lawyers are well-versed in handling personal injury cases.

Furthermore, our firm boasts a wealth of experience in representing clients in various criminal matters nationwide, appearing in all courts, including the Supreme Court of India, High Courts, and industry courts. Our team comprises efficient professionals and qualified advocates who excel in handling criminal and quasi-criminal cases, such as defamation, white-collar crimes, economic offenses, insider trading, corporate fraud, market manipulation, and more. We are fully equipped to manage the critical aspects and consequences of criminal litigation, conducting thorough investigations and offering optimal solutions to ensure fair trials.

Practice Area

Corporate & Commercial Dispute

The firm boasts a robust litigation practice and a well-established foundation in various dispute resolution mechanisms, particularly focusing on commercial disputes. Our primary objective is to ensure that conflicts are resolved amicably and cost-effectively.

Covering a diverse spectrum of industries including telecommunications, aviation, energy, manufacturing, natural resources, insurance, print media, hospitality, financial services, health, entertainment, intellectual property, employment, information technology, retail and trade, property and construction, our team offers comprehensive legal advice and representation to a wide array of local and international businesses.

With a proven track record of handling landmark cases and extensive experience in navigating complex litigation scenarios, our firm is equipped to tackle even the most challenging legal disputes. We are committed to delivering optimal outcomes for our clients by providing strategic counsel and expert representation throughout the dispute resolution process.

Practice Area

Construction & Real Estate

As a leading Real Estate Law firm, we excel in providing specialized services specifically focused on Land Laws. Our dedicated team of legal experts offers comprehensive guidance and support on all aspects of legal and regulatory matters pertaining to land ownership, investment, development, and utilization.

We cater to a diverse clientele, including landowners, investors, funds, and developers, offering tailored legal advice and strategic solutions to address their unique needs and challenges. Our extensive experience and in-depth understanding of local land revenue laws enable us to navigate complex legal landscapes effectively.

Over the years, our firm has successfully assisted clients in resolving a myriad of issues related to land, including disputes, acquisitions, disposals, joint ventures, real estate funds and investments, trust advice, and real estate finance. Whether it’s advising on land acquisition strategies, negotiating joint venture agreements, structuring real estate transactions, or providing guidance on regulatory compliance, our team is dedicated to delivering practical and effective solutions to our clients.

Our commitment to excellence and client satisfaction is reflected in our proactive approach, attention to detail, and unwavering dedication to achieving favorable outcomes. With our comprehensive legal expertise and industry knowledge, we are proud to be a trusted partner for all land-related legal matters.

Practice Area

RERA

In the realm of RERA-related affairs, our legal practice stands out as one of the most sought-after in Delhi, NCR. With profound expertise in real estate law and its prospective developments, our adept attorneys maintain a comprehensive understanding of the financial landscape. We cater to a diverse clientele including property funds, banks, developer conglomerates, and affluent individuals. Many of India’s largest real estate enterprises rely on referrals from our real estate lawyers for assistance with joint ventures, targeted funds, future investments, property transactions, leases, and more. Our team possesses the proficiency to navigate intricate real estate matters adeptly.

We provide comprehensive support to lenders, investors, and real estate developers across all facets of transactions, encompassing financing, debt restructuring, and disposition issues. Our attorneys are well-versed in the nuances of the real estate sector, including the opportunities and challenges presented by RERA. Our dedicated team has recently achieved significant relief for homebuyers seeking legal recourse through various forums, including RERA Benches and Consumer Forums at multiple levels.

Practice Area

Banking, Recovery and Debts

The firm is dedicated to providing a comprehensive array of services tailored to meet the needs of borrowers, lenders, advisors, and other intermediaries across various sectors. Our esteemed clientele includes a wide spectrum of entities such as financial institutions, prominent banks, Non-Banking Financial Companies (NBFCs), and commercial enterprises.

One of our key strengths lies in our ability to navigate complex negotiations and structural issues with finesse. Our experienced team is adept at managing the entire spectrum of tasks involved in financial transactions. This includes conducting thorough due diligence on promoters and lenders, meticulously structuring transactions to ensure optimal outcomes, drafting comprehensive term sheets, skillfully negotiating agreements, preparing precise transactional documents, offering insightful regulatory guidance, and addressing any related concerns that may arise throughout the process.

In the realm of banking and financial industries, we have established ourselves as industry leaders, thanks to our unparalleled expertise and track record of success. Our dedicated group of professionals possesses extensive experience and a deep understanding of the intricacies of the sector. Over the years, we have proudly assisted numerous clients with a wide range of crucial banking and financial services. This includes but is not limited to facilitating project financing, orchestrating acquisition financing, spearheading debt restructuring initiatives, and orchestrating syndicated lending arrangements, among other endeavors.

At our firm, we are committed to delivering superior service and achieving exceptional results for our clients in the dynamic and ever-evolving landscape of banking and finance.
