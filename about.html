About The Firm

One of the foremost law firms in Delhi-NCR.

We, at Motani Associates, are a premier full-service and independent law firm based in Delhi-NCR, India. We are dedicated to providing comprehensive legal services to a diverse clientele, both domestic and international. Our clients include individuals, public and private companies, multinational corporations, public sector undertakings (PSUs), and other corporate entities across the country.

Our objective is to deliver holistic solutions tailored to meet the specific legal needs of our clients. Our team comprises highly qualified and responsive legal professionals, ranging from seasoned veterans to dynamic young lawyers, all of whom have been recognized and honored for their exceptional contributions in their respective fields of law.

In a relatively short period, our firm has earned a reputation as one of the foremost law firms in Delhi-NCR, India. We possess extensive experience in litigation, trials, investigations, witness examinations, pleadings, and comprehensive legal research.

Our areas of practice include, but are not limited to, Civil Law, Criminal Law, Anti-Corruption Law, laws related to economic offenses (ED/CBI/EOW), Family and Divorce Law, Adoption and Custody, Banking and Finance, Corporate and Commercial Law, Construction and Infrastructure, Dispute Resolution, Employment and Labour Law, Intellectual Property Rights, and Personal Injury.
